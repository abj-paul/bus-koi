The Challenge We're Solving

The Problem

  • DU students lose 100 hours per day due to missing buses
  • 94% of non-resident students need bus tracking
  • Messenger groups are ineffective for immediate needs
  • Hardware solutions are too costly to implement

Our Solution

  • Privacy-first crowd-sourced tracking
  • AI clustering to identify bus locations
  • Real-time notifications and alerts
  • User-friendly interface for all stakeholders
